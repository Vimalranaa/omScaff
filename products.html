CUPLOCK

Cuplock scaffolding is a type of scaffolding characterized by a joining method that uses metal cups to lock beams together. Cuplock scaffolding is a temporary network of frames used to support workers during construction of high access points. The overall stability and ease of assembly of the cuplock system makes it the preferred type for projects where a large scaffolding must be constructed quickly and must support a relatively heavy load.
