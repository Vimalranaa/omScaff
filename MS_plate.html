MS Plates

MS Plates are used to provide a flat, weight bearing surface for even load distribution of scaffold standards (upright tubes). It is used for joining NB tubes at 90 degrees angle to other tubes, ledgers or props. It is the water tight shuttering which can bear the load of cement concrete placed on it. It can be used for horizontal, vertical or any other shape required for the work. It gives leveled surface which has good appearance. If the plaster is required, the thickness of plaster will be less. Being water tight, the strength of concrete with steel shuttering is comparatively higher.
